Granby Colleges of Science and Technology – Naic, Cavite

The GCST Tracking System serves as a digital monitoring and management platform for Granby Colleges of Science and Technology, supporting students, faculty, and administrative staff across all levels of education—from elementary to college. As part of a private, non-sectarian institution recognized by CHED, TESDA, and DepEd, the system is designed to streamline the tracking, recording, and management of institutional activities, records, and transactions. It aligns with the institution’s mission of providing efficient and accessible services that support quality education, particularly for underprivileged communities.

Developed to support the institution’s growing administrative and academic needs since its establishment in 1999, the GCST Tracking System has evolved into a centralized digital platform for monitoring and managing school-related data. The system enables efficient tracking of records, real-time data monitoring, automated reporting, and organized system management that supports various academic and administrative departments. These include programs such as Education, Information Technology, Criminology, Accountancy, Business Administration, and Tourism Management, as well as the K–12 Academic and TVL tracks.

With a strong focus on efficiency, transparency, and institutional coordination, the GCST Tracking System helps ensure that records and processes are accurately monitored and properly managed. By integrating modern digital solutions, the system allows students, faculty, and administrative staff to access essential information, monitor activities, and manage transactions more effectively. Through this platform, Granby Colleges of Science and Technology continues to strengthen its commitment to responsible management, technological advancement, and improved service delivery within the academic community.
